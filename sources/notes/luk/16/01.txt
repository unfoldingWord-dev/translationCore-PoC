===== Translation Notes: =====
  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus begins telling another parable. It is about a master and the manager of his debtors. This is still the same part of the story and the same day that begin in [[:en:bible:notes:luk:15:03|15:3]].

  * **Jesus also said to the disciples** - The last section was directed at Pharisees and scribes, though Jesus' disciples may have been part of the crowd listening.
  * **There was a certain rich man** - This is the beginning of another parable. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]])
  * **it was reported to him** - AT: "people reported to the rich man" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **squandering his possessions** - "foolishly spending the rich man's wealth"
  * **What is this that I hear about you?** - The rich man uses a question to scold the manager. AT: "I have heard what you are doing." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Give an account of your management** - AT: "Set things in order to pass on to someone else" or ""Prepare the records you have written about my money"
