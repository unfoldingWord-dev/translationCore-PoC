===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It came about that** - This phrase is used here to mark an event in the story. If your language has a way for doing this, you could consider using it here.
  * **was carried away by the angels** - AT: "the angels carried him away" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **to Abraham's side** - Apparently Abraham and Lazarus were reclining next to each other at a feast in heaven. This was the Greek style of feasting for invited guests. AT: "to sit next to Abraham" or "to recline next to Abraham."
  * **and was buried** - AT: "and people buried him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **and in hades, being in torment** - AT: "and he went to hades where he was suffering in terrible pain"
  * **he lifted up his eyes** - AT: "he looked up" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **and Lazarus against his chest** - AT: "and Lazarus reclining next to Abraham" or "and Lazarus with him"
