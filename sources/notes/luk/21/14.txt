===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Therefore** - "Because of this." Jesus uses this word here to refer back to everything he has said, beginning in [[:en:bible:notes:luk:21:10|21:10]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_connectingwords]])
  * **resolve in your heart** - AT: "make up your mind" or "decide firmly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **not to prepare your defense** - "not to figure out what you will say in order to defend yourself against their accusations"
  * **I will give you words and wisdom** - "I will tell you what wise things to say"
  * **words and wisdom** - AT: "words of wisdom" or "wise words" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hendiadys]])
  * **which all your adversaries will not be able to resist or contradict** - This can be translated as a new sentence. AT: "Your adversaries will not be able to argue against you or say that you are wrong" or "Your enemies must agree that you are right."
