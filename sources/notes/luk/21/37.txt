===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - This is the end of the part of the story that began in [[:en:bible:notes:luk:21:01|20:1]]. These verses tell on-going action that continues after the main part of the story ends. (See: [[End of Story]])

  * **during the days he was teaching** - "during the days he would teach." The following verses tell about things that Jesus and the people did each day during the week before he died.
  * **in the temple** - AT: "at the temple" or "in the temple courtyard" 
  * **at night he went out** - "at night he would go out of the city" or "he went out each night"
  * **All of the people** - AT: "a very large number of people" or "almost everyone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **came early in the morning** - "would come early in the morning" or "came early each morning"
  * **to hear him** - "to hear him teach"
