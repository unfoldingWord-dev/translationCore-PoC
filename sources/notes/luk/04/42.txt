===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Though the people wanted Jesus to stay in Capernaum, he goes to preach in other Judean synagogues.

  * **When daybreak came** - "At sunrise" or "At dawn"
  * **a solitary place** - "a deserted place" or "a place where there were no people"
  * **to many other cities** - "to the people in many other cities"
  * **this is the reason I was sent here** - This could be stated in active form. AT: "this is the reason God sent me here." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Judea** - Since Jesus had been in Galilee, the term "Judea" here probably refers to the entire region where the Jews lived at that time. AT: "where the Jews lived."
