===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Jesus said to them, "When you pray say, 'Father, sanctify your name** - Jesus, the Son of God, commanded the disciples to honor the name of God "the Father" by addressing God as, "the Father," when praying.
  * **Jesus said to them** - "Jesus said to his disciples"
  * **Father** - This is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **sanctify your name** - AT: "may all people honor you" or "cause everyone to honor your name" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Let your kingdom come** - "Establish your kingdom." AT: "We want you to rule over your people." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
