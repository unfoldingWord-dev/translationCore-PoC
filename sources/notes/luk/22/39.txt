===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the next part of the story. Jesus got to the Mount of Olives to pray.

  * **After supper** - This refers to the completion of the Passover meal. 
  * **that you do not enter into temptation** - "that you are not tempted" or "that nothing tempts you and causes you to sin"
