===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **about a stone's throw** - "about the distance that someone can throw a stone." AT: "a short distance" or with an estimated measurement like "about 30 meters" (UDB). (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Father, if you are willing** - Jesus will bear the guilt of all the sin of human history by the agony of the cross. He prays to his Father asking if there is another way.
  * **Father** - This is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **remove this cup from me** - Jesus is referring to the suffering that he is about to endure as if it were in a cup and that he was going to drink it. AT: "take away from me this cup of suffering" or "take away this suffering from me" or "rescue me from having to suffer like this." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Nevertheless not my will, but yours be done** - AT: "However, I want to do what is according to your will rather than what is according to my will" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
