===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus continues speaking to the Pharisee who had invited him to his home.

  * **you will be blessed** - AT: "God will bless you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **they cannot repay you** - AT: "they cannot invite you to a banquet in return"
  * **you will be repaid in the resurrection of the just** - AT: "God will repay you when he brings the righteous people back to life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
