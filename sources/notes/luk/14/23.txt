===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus finishes his parable.

  * **the highways and hedges** - This refers to roads and paths outside of the city. AT: "the main roads and paths outside of the city."
  * **compel them** - "demand them"
  * **that my house may be filled** - "so that people may fill my house"
  * **For I say to you** - The word "you" refers to the servant.
  * **those men** - The word here for "men" means "male adults" and not just people in general.
  * **who were first invited** - AT: "whom I first invited" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **will taste my dinner** - "will enjoy the dinner I have prepared"
