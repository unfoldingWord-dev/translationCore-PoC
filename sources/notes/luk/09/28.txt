===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It came about**  - This phrase is used here to mark an important event in the story. If your language has a way for doing this, you could consider using it here.
  * **these words**  - This refers to what Jesus said to his disciples in the preceding verses.
  * **into the mountain**  - It is unclear how far up the mountain they went. AT: "on the side of a mountain." 
  * **white and dazzling**  - "shining white and brilliant" or "brilliant white and shining" or "dazzling white and shining like lightening" (UDB).
