===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - This is the next event in this part of the story. Jesus enters the temple.

  * **cast out** - "throw out" or "force out"
  * **It is written** - This is a quotation from Isaiah. AT: "The scriptures say" or "A prophet wrote these words in the scriptures." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **My house** - The word "My" refers to God and "house" refers to the temple.
  * **house of prayer** - "a place where people pray to me"
  * **a den of robbers** - "a place where robbers hide out." AT: "like a den of robbers." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
