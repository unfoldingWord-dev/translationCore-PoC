===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the next part of the story. Zacchaeus is introduced to the story. Verse 1 gives background information about Jesus' travels. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **Behold, there was a man there** - The word "behold" alerts us to a new person in the story. Your language may have a way of doing this. AT: "There was a man who was"
  * **He was a chief tax collector and was rich** - This is background information about Zacchaeus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
