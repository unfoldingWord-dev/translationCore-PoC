===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **As he was now drawing near** - "As Jesus was going near" or "as Jesus was approaching." Jesus' disciples were traveling with him.
  * **mighty works which they had seen** - AT: "great things they had seen Jesus do"
  * **Blessed is the king** - They were saying this about Jesus.
  * **in the name of the Lord** - Here "name" refers to power and authority. Also, "Lord" refers to God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **glory in the highest** - AT: "let everyone give glory to the One in the highest" or "let everyone praise the Highest One"
