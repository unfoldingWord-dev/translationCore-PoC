===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **When I was with you** - "When I was with you before"
  * **all that was written in the law of Moses and the prophets and the Psalms** - AT: "all that Moses, the prophets, and the writers of the Psalms wrote about me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **must be fulfilled** - AT: "God would fulfill" or "God would cause to happen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
