===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The two men continue responding to Jesus.

  * **who would free Israel** - The Romans ruled over the Jews. AT: "who would free the Israelites from our Roman enemies." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the third day** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]]
  * **since these things came to pass** - "since they killed him"
