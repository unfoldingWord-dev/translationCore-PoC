===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I say to you my friends** - - Jesus readdresses his audience to mark a shift in his speech to a new topic, in this case, to speak about not being afraid.
  * **they have no more that they can do** - "they cannot cause any more harm" or "they cannot hurt you anymore"
  * **Fear the one who** - AT: "Fear God who" or "Fear God, because"
  * **after he has killed** - "after he kills you" or "after he kills someone"
  * **has authority to throw you into hell** - AT: "has authority to throw people into hell"
