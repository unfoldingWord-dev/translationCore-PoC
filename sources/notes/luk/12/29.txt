===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Do not look for what you will eat and what you will drink** - "Do not focus on what you will eat and drink" or "Do not greatly desire more to eat and drink"
  * **all the nations of the world** - Here "nations" refers to "unbelievers." AT: "all people of other nations" or "all the unbelievers in the world." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **your Father knows that you need these things** - Jesus' Father, God the Father, also becomes the Father of those who believe in Jesus.
  * **Father** - This is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
