===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Blessed are** - "How good it is for"
  * **whom the master will find watching when he comes** - "whose master finds them waiting for him when he returns" or "who are ready when the master returns"
  * **he will tuck...make them sit down** - Because the servants have been faithful and ready to serve their master, the master will now reward them by serving them.
  * **he will tuck his long robe in his belt** - AT: "he will prepare to serve them by tucking his clothes into his belt" or "he will dress to serve" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **in the second watch of the night** - The second watch was between 9:00 p.m. and midnight. AT: "late at night" or "just before midnight."
  * **or if even in the third watch** - The third watch was from midnight to 3:00 a.m. AT: "or if he comes very late at night."
