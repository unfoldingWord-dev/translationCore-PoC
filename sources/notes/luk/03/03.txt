===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **preaching a baptism of repentance** - The terms "baptism" and "repentance" could be stated as actions. AT: "preached that people should be baptized to show that they were repenting." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **for the forgiveness of sins** - "so that their sins would be forgiven" or "so that God would forgive their sins." The repentance was for the forgiveness of sins.
