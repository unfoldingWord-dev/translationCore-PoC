===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Mary, Joseph, and Jesus leave the town of Bethlehem and return to the city of Nazareth for his childhood.

  * **they were required to do according to the law of the Lord** - This could be stated in active form. AT: "that the law of the Lord required them to do." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **their own city, Nazareth** - This phrase means they lived in Nazareth. Make sure it does not sound like they owned the city.
  * **increasing in wisdom** - "became more wise" or "learned what was wise"
  * **the grace of God was upon him** - "God blessed him" or "God was with him in a special way"
