===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **my eyes have seen** - This expression means, "I have personally seen" or "I, myself, have seen." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **your salvation** - This expression refers to the person who would bring salvation—the infant Jesus—whom Simeon was holding. AT: "the savior whom you sent" or "the one whom you sent to save" (UDB). (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **which you** - Depending on how you translate the previous phrase, this may need to be changed to "whom you."
  * **have prepared** - "have planned" or "caused to happen"
  * **in the sight of all peoples** - "for all people groups to see"
  * **He is light** - This metaphor means that the child will help people to see and understand the say way light helps people see accurately. AT: "this child will enable people to understand as light allows people to see accurately." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **for revelation** - It be necessary to state what is to be revealed. AT: "that will reveal God's truth." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **glory of your people Israel** - "he will be the reason that glory will come to your people Israel"
