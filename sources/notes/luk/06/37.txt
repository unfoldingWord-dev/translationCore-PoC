===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Do not judge** - "Do not judge people" or "Do not harshly criticize people" (UDB)
  * **and you** - "and as a result you"
  * **you will not be judged** - Jesus did not say who would not judge. Possible meanings are 1) "God will not judge you" or 2) "no one will judge you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Do not condemn** - "Do not condemn people"
  * **you will not be condemned** - Jesus did not say who would not condemn. Possible meanings are 1) "God will not condemn you" or 2) "no one will condemn you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **you will be forgiven** - Jesus did not say who would forgive. Possible meanings are 1) "God will forgive you" or 2) "people will forgive you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
