===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Or those** - This is Jesus' second example of people who suffered. AT: "Or consider those" or "Think about those."
  * **eighteen people** - "18 people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Siloam** - This is the name of an area in Jerusalem. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **do you think they were worse sinners** - "were they more sinful" or "does this prove that they were more sinful?" Jesus uses a question to teach the people. AT: "do not think that they were more sinful." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **other men** - "other people"
  * **No, I say** - Here "I say" emphasizes "No." AT: "They certainly were not more sinful" or "You are wrong to think that their suffering proves that they were more sinful." 
  * **perish** - "lose your life" or "die"
