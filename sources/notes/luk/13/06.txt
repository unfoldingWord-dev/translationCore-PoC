===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Jesus begins to tell a parable to explain his last statement, "But if you do not repent, all of you will also perish." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]])

  * **Someone had a fig tree planted** - AT: "A person planted a fig tree" (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **three years** - "3 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Why let it waste the ground?** - The man uses a question to emphasize that the tree is useless and the gardener should cut it down. AT: "Do not let it waste the ground." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
