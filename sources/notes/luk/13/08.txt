===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus finishes telling his parable. This is the end of the part of the story that began in [[:en:bible:notes:luk:12:01|12:1]]. 

  * **Leave it alone** - AT: "Do not do anything to the tree" or "Do not cut it down" 
  * **and put manure on it** - "and put manure in the soil." Manure is animal dung. People put it in the ground to make the soil good for plants and trees.
  * **cut it down** - The servant was making a suggestion; he was not giving a command to the owner. AT: "Let me cut it down" or "Tell me to cut it down."
