===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he added** - "the criminal also said"
  * **come into your kingdom** - AT: "begin to rule as king" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Truly I say to you** - "Truly" adds emphasis to what Jesus is saying. AT: "I want you to know that."
  * **paradise** - This is the place that righteous people go to when they die. Jesus was assuring the man that he would be with God and God would accept him. AT: "the happy place" or "the place of the righteous" or "the place where people live well."
